WIRE DRAWING MACHINE

Straight line wire drawing machines are used for reducing wire or wire rods diameter by stretching or drawing them through various size dies without breaking them and to attain smooth finishing of the required size of wire. Straight line wire drawing machines are free of any limitations as far as material grade is concerned.

ACCESSORIES FOR THE WIRE-DRAWING PLANT

BUTT WELDING MACHINE

BUTT WELDING MACHINE is used for welding the two ends of cut wire during the continuous Wire drawing process. Both the ends of cut wire can be joined immediately for faster drawing process.

  • Fixed & moveable platens are compactly constructed from high-grade aluminium castings
  • Precision parallel guides ensuring frictionless movement.
  • Special wear resisting copper alloy electrodes with precision V-grooves.
  • Automatic welding cycle. The current gets cut off at a pre-set upsetting distance.

WIRE POINTING MACHINE

Wire Pointing Machine is used to make the wire point to easily pass through wire drawing die. It basically consists of two high carbon alloy steel rollers with reducing grooves working on bronze metal bushes mounted on the frame. Wire Pointing Machine is equipped with 3 HP motor.

pit type ELECTRIC FURNACE

Pit type of Electric furnace is used for heat treatment of semi-finished wire products to change its mechanical properties. Usually annealing is done on 2.5 mm wire before further reduction in wire diameter, to soften the MS wire material. Annealing is carried out to soften wire material and hence an industrial furnace is an in integral part of a wire drawing plant. Pit furnaces are suitable for a wide range of heat treat applications. The circular cross-section of RO elements gives high long-term stability with Minimum resistance change. The elements completely surround the work load providing effective heat transfer Efficiency. The Furnace is top loading with an O-ring sealed chamber. The quick heating rate, heavy load capacity, and reliable operation help you to achieve better productivity and smooth operation
