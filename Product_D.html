HB WIRE

Hard Bright wire is used in a wide variety of industrial applications. It is different from regular wire products. It is widely used for wire nail and screw manufacturing. First wire is de-scaled and the further process is done by wire drawing through multiple dies to achieve desired dimensions through cold processing. It comes in various standard sizes ranging from 6 SWG to 14 SWG.
